Recent Activities
  • Youth Empowerment Workshop - June 2024

    We held a Youth Empowerment Workshop focusing on skill-building and personal development for teenagers. The event included sessions on leadership, career planning, and mental health awareness.

  • Senior Support Program - April 2024

    In April, we launched our Senior Support Program, providing essential services such as grocery delivery, transportation assistance, and companionship to elderly residents.

  • Educational Outreach Campaign - March 2024

    Our Educational Outreach Campaign in March focused on raising awareness about the importance of education in underprivileged areas. We distributed educational materials, organized tutoring sessions, and worked with local schools to support students' academic growth.

  • Food Distribution Drive - July 2024

    In July, we organized a food distribution drive to support families facing food insecurity. With the help of our volunteers and generous donations from local businesses, we distributed over 2,000 meals to those in need.
